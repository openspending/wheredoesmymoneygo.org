What is this?

Where Does My Money Go? aims to promote transparency and citizen engagement through the analysis and visualisation of information about UK public spending.

How can I get in touch?

To contact us via email write to info@okfn.org. For more contact options see the contact page on the Open Knowledge Foundation website.

Where Does My Money Go? is based on OpenSpending, where you can also find the source data.
This is an Open Knowledge Foundation project, developed with generous support from Channel 4's 4iP.
